## Purpose

The Observability layer provides **visibility into data health, freshness, and pipeline reliability** across the CMS lakehouse.

---

## Responsibilities

- Monitor data quality metrics
- Track pipeline execution status
- Detect data freshness and volume anomalies
- Support operational troubleshooting

---

## Observability Metrics

- Record counts by dataset and layer
- Freshness and latency indicators
- Data quality check results
- Pipeline success/failure rates

---

## Design Principles

- Observability is built-in, not an afterthought
- Metrics are reproducible and auditable
- Quality signals are actionable

---

## Consumers

- Data engineers
- Platform operators
- Governance and audit processes
