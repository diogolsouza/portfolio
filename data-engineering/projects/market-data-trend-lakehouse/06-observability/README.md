## Purpose

The Observability layer provides **visibility into data health, freshness, and pipeline reliability** across the market data platform.

---

## Responsibilities

- Monitor data quality metrics
- Track pipeline execution and latency
- Detect data gaps and anomalies
- Support operational troubleshooting

---

## Observability Metrics

- Record counts by asset and timeframe
- Freshness and latency indicators
- Data quality validation results
- Pipeline success and failure rates

---

## Design Principles

- Observability is built-in, not retrofitted
- Metrics are reproducible and auditable
- Signals are actionable and transparent

---

## Consumers

- Data engineers
- Platform operators
- Architecture and governance reviews
